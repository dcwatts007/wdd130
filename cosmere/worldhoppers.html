Hoid

Hoid (Wit): Hoid is one of the most enigmatic and prominent worldhoppers in the Cosmere. He appears in many of Sanderson's books, often under different names and disguises. He has been known as Wit in "The Stormlight Archive" and other names in different books. Hoid possesses the ability to manipulate light and investiture, which allows him to travel between worlds. He is on a quest to collect different forms of magic and knowledge from various planets.
